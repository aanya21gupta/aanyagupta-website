Service
Nonprofit · Points of Light Award
Dancing Hearts Project
Founder & President / CEO · Grades 10–12
Founded Dancing Hearts Project with a mission to bring intergenerational connection and the mental and physical benefits of dance to seniors and physically challenged individuals. Born from at-home dance parties with my grandparents, the idea has grown into a national nonprofit spanning 5 states, with 27+ volunteers, 46+ partner centers, and 1,000+ seniors served. I lead all strategic partnerships and advisor relationships (6 partnerships, 8 advisors), developed the organization's long-term growth vision, and personally teach dance classes at senior living communities including Saratoga Retirement Community, Priya Living, MorningStar, and Chateau Cupertino. I choreograph routines tailored for students 60+ — both standing and seated — across Bollywood, fusion, and classic styles. Recognized by Senator Dave Cortese, Representative Ro Khanna, Assemblymember Alex Lee, Mayor Lisa Gillmor, and Councilmember Raj Chahal.
